Swift, Deadly, and Unpredictable

Faction Description

The Raiders are the masters of the seas and coastal regions, striking from the water with lightning speed and disappearing before their enemies can respond. Born from the harsh coastal lands where survival depends on cunning and speed, the Raiders have perfected the art of hit-and-run warfare. Their ships cut through the waves like blades, while their warriors strike with the ferocity of a storm.

Playstyle

Hit-and-Run Tactics: The Raiders excel at quick strikes, naval superiority, and mobility. They avoid prolonged engagements, preferring to strike hard and fast before retreating to safety.

Difficulty

Intermediate: Requires good positioning and timing. Players need to understand when to strike and when to retreat, making them more challenging than defensive factions.

Faction Passives & Buffs

  • Sea Mastery: All units gain +2 Movement when on water hexes
  • Raid Tactics: +25% Gold gained from destroying enemy units
  • Coastal Advantage: +1 Attack when fighting adjacent to water
  • Swift Strike: Units can move after attacking once per turn

Strategy Suggestions

  • Early Game: Secure coastal territories and build naval superiority
  • Mid Game: Use hit-and-run tactics to weaken enemy positions
  • Late Game: Coordinate naval and land strikes for decisive victories
  • Key Units: Marauders for naval combat, Hunters for land strikes, Ballista for siege support
