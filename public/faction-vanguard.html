Honor, Courage, and Balanced Warfare

Faction Description

The Vanguard represents the pinnacle of balanced warfare, combining the best aspects of all military disciplines. As the most versatile faction, they excel in both offense and defense, making them the perfect choice for players who want flexibility in their strategies. Their knights charge with honor, their archers rain death from afar, and their leaders inspire their troops to greatness.

Playstyle

Balanced Warfare: The Vanguard excels at adapting to any situation. They can switch between aggressive and defensive strategies seamlessly, making them unpredictable and versatile on the battlefield.

Difficulty

Beginner-Friendly: Their balanced nature makes them forgiving for new players while still offering depth for experienced commanders who want tactical flexibility.

Faction Passives & Buffs

  • Adaptive Tactics: Units gain +1 to their lowest stat each turn
  • Honor Bound: Cannot retreat but gain +2 Defense when outnumbered
  • Versatile Training: All units cost 5% less resources
  • Inspiring Presence: Adjacent friendly units gain +1 Attack

Strategy Suggestions

  • Early Game: Build a balanced army with both ranged and melee units
  • Mid Game: Adapt your strategy based on enemy weaknesses
  • Late Game: Use your versatility to counter enemy strategies
  • Key Units: Knights for heavy combat, Archers for ranged support, Dukes for leadership
