Order, Discipline, and Conquest

Faction Description

The Empire stands as the bastion of order and civilization in a world torn by chaos. With their disciplined legions, advanced siege technology, and unwavering determination, the Empire seeks to bring all lands under their banner through superior tactics and overwhelming force. Their armies march with precision, their walls stand unbreakable, and their leaders command with the wisdom of centuries of military tradition.

Playstyle

Defensive Expansion: The Empire excels at fortifying positions and expanding methodically. Their strong defensive capabilities allow them to hold territory while building up for decisive offensives.

Difficulty

Beginner-Friendly: The Empire's straightforward mechanics and balanced units make them an excellent choice for new players. Their defensive nature provides a forgiving learning curve.

Faction Passives & Buffs

  • Imperial Discipline: All units gain +1 Defense when defending
  • Siege Mastery: Siege units deal +25% damage to structures
  • Legion Training: Units cost 10% less Gold to recruit
  • Fortress Construction: Buildings cost 15% less Iron to construct

Strategy Suggestions

  • Early Game: Focus on securing defensive positions and building economic infrastructure
  • Mid Game: Construct siege weapons and begin methodical expansion
  • Late Game: Use overwhelming siege power to crush enemy strongholds
  • Key Units: Legionnaires for defense, Siege Towers for offense, Imperial Guard for elite operations
