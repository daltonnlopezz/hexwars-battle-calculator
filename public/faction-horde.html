Faction Description

The HORDE represents the raw power of nature and the unbridled fury of the wild. These savage warriors fight with animalistic ferocity, overwhelming their enemies through sheer numbers and relentless aggression. Their connection to the primal forces of the world makes them unpredictable and devastating in combat, striking fear into the hearts of their enemies.

Playstyle

Overwhelming Aggression: The HORDE excels at rushing enemies with superior numbers and raw power. They sacrifice defense for overwhelming offense, crushing enemies through sheer force and intimidation.

Difficulty

Intermediate: Requires aggressive playstyle and good timing. Players need to understand when to strike and how to manage their high-damage, low-defense units effectively.

Faction Passives & Buffs

  • Berserker Rage: +2 Attack when below 50% health
  • Pack Tactics: +1 Attack for each adjacent friendly unit
  • Intimidation: Enemy units lose -1 Defense when adjacent
  • Wild Fury: Cannot be routed or intimidated

Strategy Suggestions

  • Early Game: Rush enemy positions with overwhelming numbers
  • Mid Game: Use pack tactics to maximize damage output
  • Late Game: Overwhelm with elite units and berserker rage
  • Key Units: Bearzerkers for raw power, Yotes for numbers, Warships for naval dominance
