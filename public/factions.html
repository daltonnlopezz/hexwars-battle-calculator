FACTIONS OF HEX WARS

Introduction to HEX Wars
Welcome to the battlefield, where each faction offers a unique experience and challenges for those who seek to lead them to victory. As a new player, you'll have the opportunity to command armies, master strategies, and forge your path to dominance.

Whether you prefer the balanced might of the Vanguard, the swift and deadly tactics of the Raiders, or the relentless assault of the HORDE, there is a faction perfectly suited to your playstyle.

Below you will find each page dedicated to a new faction to dominate the landscape. Each Faction will have differing playstyles and units to command. You will also find abilities unique to the HEX Wars Lore. Choose your faction wisely, for your decisions will shape the course of history.

For those who seek unyielding resilience and overwhelming power, the Ironborne stand as your unbreakable hammer to dominate your foes. Seize your destiny and forge your legacy with the might of the Terrador elite. As the most defensive faction, the Ironborne Dwarves are slow but nearly unstoppable once they reach the battlefield. üíÄ Damned
Arise, forsaken one, for death has called upon you to spread his shadow across the lands. No longer bound by the frailties of mortal life, you are reborn as the harbinger of doom, the dark general of the Damned. The cold grip of death is no end, but a beginning‚Äîyour will is now eternal, your power unyielding. As a servant of the Damned, you are destined to lead our legion of the fallen. The Damned are unlike any other faction, embodying death and decay with a unique playstyle that will challenge even the most experienced commanders. As the only non-living race, the Damned have no supply limits, and their units cost nothing to muster, as they are harvested from the earth itself. The Damned spread their influence by erecting Harvesters to claim surrounding territory and expand their undead legions. The Harvester siege unit is the cornerstone of their strategy. Construct a harvester by consuming basic units which in turn will spawn more undead soldiers. Their special unit, the Lost Soul, emerges from the fallen bodies of the living, no matter where they die across the battlefield. With leaders who spread plague or resurrect fallen enemies, the Damned grow stronger as the world around them dies. Your army awaits your command, hungering for souls to fuel our unholy march of doom.
