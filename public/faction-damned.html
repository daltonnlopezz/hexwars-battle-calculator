💀 THE DAMNED
Death, Decay, and Unholy Power

Faction Description

The Damned are the masters of death and decay, risen from the grave to spread their unholy influence across the land. As the only non-living faction, they have no supply limits and their units cost nothing to muster, as they are harvested from the earth itself. Their power grows as the world around them dies, making them stronger with each battle fought and each life extinguished.

Playstyle

Scaling & Resource-Free: The Damned excel at growing stronger over time and overwhelming enemies with free units. They spread their influence by erecting Harvesters and converting the fallen into their undead legions.

Difficulty

Expert: Requires deep understanding of the game's mechanics and timing. Players need to manage their unique resource system and understand when to strike for maximum impact.

Faction Passives & Buffs

  • Undead Nature: All units cost 0 Food, Gold, and Iron
  • Harvest Power: Gain 1 Other resource per turn per Harvester
  • Death Growth: +1 Other resource for each enemy unit killed
  • Unholy Resilience: Units cannot be routed or intimidated

Strategy Suggestions

  • Early Game: Build Harvesters to generate Other resources
  • Mid Game: Use free units to overwhelm enemy positions
  • Late Game: Deploy elite units powered by accumulated Other resources
  • Key Units: Harvesters for resource generation, Lost Souls for unit conversion, Plaguebringers for area control
