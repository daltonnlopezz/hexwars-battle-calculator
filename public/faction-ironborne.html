Forged in Fire, Tempered by War

Faction Description

The Ironborne are masters of metal and stone, forged in the depths of the earth and hardened by centuries of warfare. Their connection to the mountains and mines gives them unparalleled access to resources and the knowledge to craft the finest weapons and armor. They are slow but unstoppable, like a mountain avalanche, crushing everything in their path with superior technology and unbreakable determination.

Playstyle

Heavy Industry & Defense: The Ironborne excel at building powerful defensive positions and overwhelming enemies with superior technology. They are slow to mobilize but devastating when they strike, using their advanced weapons and armor to dominate the battlefield.

Difficulty

Advanced: Requires strategic planning and resource management. Players need to understand the value of patience and positioning, as the Ironborne's strength comes from their preparation and technology.

Faction Passives & Buffs

  • Mountain Mastery: +2 Defense when fighting on hills or mountains
  • Iron Forge: All units gain +1 Defense and +1 Attack
  • Resource Efficiency: Buildings cost 20% less Iron to construct
  • Siege Mastery: Siege units deal +50% damage to structures

Strategy Suggestions

  • Early Game: Secure mountainous terrain and build resource infrastructure
  • Mid Game: Construct advanced siege weapons and heavy armor
  • Late Game: Overwhelm enemies with superior technology and unbreakable defenses
  • Key Units: Guardians for defense, Trebuchets for siege, Death Knights for elite combat
